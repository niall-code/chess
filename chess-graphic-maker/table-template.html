To place a chess piece on the chessboard:

  1. Nest an i element in the appropriate table cell.
  2. Give the i element a class attribute.
  3. Give the class attribute two values:
    • either fa-regular for a white piece or fa-solid for a black piece
    • and fa-chess-piece (replacing piece with the standard name of the wanted piece, i.e., rook, knight, bishop, queen, king, or pawn).
